Maps

This page provides access to detailed prevalence maps of the biggest five diseases in Scotland by National Health Service (NHS Scotland) region. The project was developed by Team 50 as part of the Python Programming Course at the University of Edinburgh. Each map features bubbles centered at the primary NHS locations, with sizes proportional to the prevalence rates within each region. Feel free to contact us regarding any questions.
